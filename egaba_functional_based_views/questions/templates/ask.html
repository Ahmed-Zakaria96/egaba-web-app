How To Ask

We beleive that the best person to answer a question will be the one with most knowledge about the context of the question.

So chainning questions into specific context where they came from was our idea to help find more appropriate answers.

Steps:
  1. Chain your question to the right context.
  2. Add appropriate descriptive title.
  3. Try explaining as best as possible:
    • The problem.
    • What are you trying to acheive.
